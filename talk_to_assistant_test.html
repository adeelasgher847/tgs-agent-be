🚀 How to Test

  1. Click any "Talk to [Agent]" button above to open the voice chat interface
  2. Allow microphone access when prompted by your browser
  3. Click "Connect" to establish the WebSocket connection
  4. Click the microphone button to start talking
  5. Speak clearly into your microphone
  6. Listen to the AI response - it will be spoken back to you
  7. Continue the conversation by clicking the microphone button again
